## Getting Started

### 1. Sign In

1. Visit the MyApps homepage
2. Click the "Sign in with Google" button
3. Select your Google account
4. Grant permissions when prompted

**Note**: Your reading data is securely stored and synced across all your devices when you sign in with Google.

### 2. Navigate to Read Tracker

Once signed in, click on the "Read Tracker" card on the homepage to start tracking your reading.

## Features Guide

### Dashboard

The Dashboard provides an overview of your reading activity:

- **Today's Reading**: Total reading time for today
- **This Week**: Weekly reading summary
- **This Month**: Monthly reading summary
- **Reading Streak**: Consecutive days with reading sessions
- **Average Reading Time**: Daily averages for the week and month
- **Books Statistics**: Count of books by status
- **Active Goals**: Progress on your reading goals
- **Charts**: Visual representation of your reading trends

### Reading Sessions

Track your reading time by adding reading sessions:

1. Click the "Add Session" button
2. Select the date you read
3. Enter the reading duration (hours and minutes)
4. Optionally link the session to a book you're reading
5. Click "Add Session"

**Viewing Sessions**:
- Filter sessions by date (Today, This Week, This Month, This Year, or All Time)
- Edit or delete sessions by clicking the respective icons
- View "Today's Reading Time" at the top of the page

### Books

Manage your reading list:

**Adding a Book**:
1. Click "Add Book"
2. Enter the book title and author (required)
3. Add a cover image URL (optional)
4. Select the book status:
   - **Want to Read**: Books you plan to read
   - **Currently Reading**: Books you're currently reading
   - **Completed**: Books you've finished
5. Optionally add start date and completion date
6. Click "Add Book"

**Managing Books**:
- Filter books by status using the tabs
- Click on a book card to view details
- Edit or delete books using the action buttons
- Update book status from the book details modal

**Linking Sessions to Books**:
When adding a reading session, you can link it to a book you're currently reading. This helps track reading time per book.

### Goals

Set reading goals to stay motivated:

**Creating a Goal**:
1. Click "Add Goal"
2. Select goal type:
   - **Daily**: Read a certain amount each day
   - **Weekly**: Read a certain amount in a specific week
   - **Monthly**: Read a certain amount in a specific month
   - **Yearly**: Read a certain amount in a specific year
3. Set your target reading time (hours and minutes)
4. For weekly/monthly/yearly goals, select the specific period
5. Click "Create Goal"

**Tracking Progress**:
- View active goals on the Dashboard and Goals page
- See progress bars showing completion percentage
- Goals show status: In Progress, On Track, Almost There, or Completed
- Edit or delete goals as needed

### Settings

View your account information:
- Email address

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Sign In
- Ensure you're using a supported browser
- Check your internet connection
- Try clearing browser cache and cookies
- Make sure pop-ups are allowed for Google sign-in

### Data Not Syncing
- Ensure you're signed in with the same Google account
- Check your internet connection
- Refresh the page

### Sessions Not Saving
- Check that you've entered valid data (date and duration)
- Ensure you're signed in
- Check your internet connection
- Try refreshing the page

### Charts Not Displaying
- Ensure you have reading sessions logged
- Check your internet connection
- Try refreshing the page
